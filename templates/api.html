Nobel Yoo API

GET
/talk

Talk to Nobel about something. Nobel likes talking quite a bit but will lose interest if the conversation starts feeling one-sided in either direction. Some common things Nobel likes talking about are:
  • Community building and organizing
  • Deep analysis/critique of art
  • Weird art that people are making
  • Internet Culture
  • Arcane knowledge

GET
/schedule

Get Nobel's schedule. You can specify a date range to get more detailed information. If you don't specify a date range, you'll get a vague description, like "Fairly Busy", "Mostly Free", etc.

POST
/schedule

Put something on Nobel's schedule. You can use this to invite Nobel to things, or to coordinate what a good time would be to do something together. It's best to provide as much concrete information as possible - when is it, where is it, how long is it going to take, etc, etc.

Nobel is open to all sorts of events. However, Nobel is especially interested in the following types of events
  • Anything related to making music
  • Anything related to weird art
  • Parties!
  • Weird music at the club!
  • Cooking with friends!
  • Dancing!

GET
/subscribe

Get information about what topics you're subscribed to with Nobel. Nobel uses a highly sophisticated algorithm to provide you with the information he thinks is best suited for you. WARNING: Invoking this route will subject you to the mortifying ordeal of being known.

POST
/subscribe

Tell Nobel what topics you'd like to be subscribed to. You can also use this route to remove yourself from various topic subscriptions. NOTICE: You cannot unsubscribe from the following topics:
  • Commie Bullshit
  • Systems Thinking
  • The beauty of existence
  • Whatever stupid thing Nobel is annoyed by at the moment

POST
/gift

Gift something to Nobel. Most of the time, this has a limited chance of success. Nobel like to not own a lot of stuff. However, there are a few categories of things that Nobel will appreciate!
  • Food, whether it's cooked food, leftovers or ingredients. Honestly, the weirder it is, the better! Nobel loves trying out weird new foods.
  • Alcohol. Nobel has been known to have a drink occasionally and also loves to be able to offer his guests drinks!
  • Musical instruments! Nobel loves making weird sounds and anything that will help him make weird sounds will be greatly appreciated!
