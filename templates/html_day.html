What's the point of this?
Personally, HTML Day is an event that celebrates and encourages a vision of the internet that's more DIY, grassroots, and independent than the version we have now, which is largely made up of megacorporations. HTML Energy describes their goals slightly differently - "to show people that the web is still alive. And to actively make the web more human and handmade."

What is some stuff I can do for HTML Day?

  • Make a Favorites Page

  • Get ideas from a list of prompts!
