Internet Fantasy is a 24/7 audio/visual radio station. the videos are mostly just sakuga or "exceptional animation". you can find a ton simply by searching "sakuga".

The music is handled by an automatic DJ program. Each song has a start point and and end point and when the stream approaches the end of one song, it starts the next song in a way that the end point of the current song lines up with the next song.
