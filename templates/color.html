Palette Tester

What is this?

I'm endlessly trying out various color palettes to use for different websites, so I figured that, for Weird Web October I might as well make a website that makes making websites easier!

How do I use it?

Just play with the various color fields on the left to dynamically change the colors of the various fields. I've also provided a section of easy to copy CSS to move to your website!

Where can I get palettes?

I'm not a natural artist by any means so I usually just steal color palettes online. I personally really like Coolors but honestly you can just pull palettes from photographs, art or even outfits.
